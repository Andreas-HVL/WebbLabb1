About

Welcome to Ligma Pizza, where family, tradition, and passion come together to create unforgettable flavors.
Our story began three generations ago, when our founder, Nonna Maria, opened a small pizzeria with a simple dream: to bring people together through the joy of handmade, authentic pizza.
Over the years, our family has lovingly passed down her original recipes, along with a commitment to using only the freshest ingredients and time-honored techniques.
Today, we continue that legacy, blending tradition with a touch of modern creativity to serve a menu that has something for everyone.
From Nonna’s classic Margherita to bold new favorites dreamed up by her grandchildren, every pizza tells a story of love, care, and shared meals around the family table.
At Ligma Pizza, you’re not just a guest—you’re part of our family.
So come on in, grab a slice, and let us share a little taste of our family’s history with you.
